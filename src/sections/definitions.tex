    \item $\unitMatrix{N}$ -- unit row vector of all 1's having the dimension $1 \times N$.
    For example,
    \[
        \unitMatrix{5} =
        \begin{bmatrix}
            1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 1
        \end{bmatrix}
    \]

    \[
        \matrixK{N}{M} = (k^r (N-k)^r)_{0 \leq k \leq N, \; 0 \leq r \leq M}
    \]
